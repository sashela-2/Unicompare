Entry requirements

Waikato, Auckland & Otago

University of Auckland

To enter the Bachelor of Commerce at Auckland, students normally need University Entrance and an entry rank score of around 150. Some majors may have additional requirements depending on the papers selected.

  • University Entrance required
  • Rank Score: 150
  • 3 Approved Subjects
  • Literacy & Numeracy Requirements

University of Waikato

The Bachelor of Business at Waikato requires students to gain University Entrance. Waikato is often seen as one of the more accessible pathways into tertiary study while still offering a strong business programme.

  • University Entrance required
  • No Rank Score
  • 3 Approved Subjects
  • Literacy & Numeracy Requirements

University of Otago

Otago accepts students who meet University Entrance requirements. The Bachelor of Commerce gives students flexibility to explore different business disciplines during their first year.

  • University Entrance required
  • No Rank Score
  • 3 Approved Subjects
  • Literacy & Numeracy Requirements
