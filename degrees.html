Business Degrees

Compare the business programmes offered by Auckland, Waikato and Otago.

Bachelor of Commerce

University of Auckland

Auckland's Bachelor of Commerce offers a large range of majors including Accounting, Finance, Marketing and Information Systems. Students can combine majors to create a degree suited to there interests.

Duration: 3 Years

Bachelor of Business

University of Waikato

Waikato's business degree focuses on practical learning and industry engagement. Students can specialise in areas such as Human Resource Management, Finance and Strategic Management.

Duration: 3 Years

Bachelor of Commerce

University of Otago

Otago's commerce degree provides students with a broad foundation in business while allowing them to choose majors and minors from a wide range of subject areas.

Duration: 3 Years
