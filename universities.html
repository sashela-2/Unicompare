Universities

University of Auckland

The University of Auckland is New Zealand's largest university and consistently ranks among the country's top institutions. Located in the heart of Auckland, it offers a wide range of programmes and is known for its strong research output, diverse student community, and global reputation.

University of Waikato

Based in Hamilton and Tauranga, the University of Waikato is known for its innovative teaching approach and strong connections with industry. Students benefit from modern facilities, supportive learning environments, and highly regarded programmes in business, computer science, education, and environmental sciences.

University of Otago

The University of Otago is New Zealand's oldest university and is located in Dunedin. It has a strong reputation for health sciences, research excellence, and vibrant student life. Its historic campus and residential college system create a unique university experience for students from across New Zealand and around the world.
